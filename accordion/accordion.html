Which Social Media Platform are you most active on?

Do you build a program from the scratch or you make use of frameworks and libraries?

Personally, I love to build projects from the ground up but several factors can alter that such as when the client wants the project to be completed within a very short period of time. I might in that case be forced to make use of frameworks or libraries.

Do you accept payment by installments for a project?

Honestly, it depends. Quite Often though, I receive 60% of the total amount agreed for the project and when it is completed and the client is satisfied, I get my remaining 40% payment.
